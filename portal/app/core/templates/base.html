What is

Powernet, is a platform for real-time control of net loads relying on Distributed Energy Resources (DER). Powernet is built on the principle of connecting information networks to the power network (“Bits to Watts”). A Home Hub (HH) aggregates resources in a home and connects them to a Cloud Coordinator (CC). The CC optimizes power set points for each HH in order to minimize cost while increasing consumer quality of service.


What Problem Are we Solving:


The power grid is changing rapidly. Renewables are expected to exceed 50% of load by 2050. The profile of loads is changing with numerous opportunities for control. The periphery of the power network is experiencing an increased adoption of behind-the-meter resources such as distributed solar and in the future storage.

Powernet provides the platform to utilize net load control to provide grid services in a cost effective manner. It enables homes to simultaneously enjoy the benefits of dynamic pricing optimization, and revenues from grid services on the assets they choose to share. We’ll essentially minimize cost, increase quality of service, preserve stability and offer services to the grid in an economically sustainable way.
